    \ Boring path

    Its always the boring problem statements that give success with very less competition in the market.

     

    Compliance is such a boring domain, and nobody had an eye on that domain for such a long time, but look at Vanta ($4.15Billion valuation) which just automates security compliance like ISO27001, GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2 etc., most of us dont even know what these are, but these are highly funded sectors in any enterprise. Nobody talks about it rarely do you see these products on producthunt or X(twitter), its only when you see an article on techcruch you realise.

    \ AI might shape real Engineers

    With AI easily taking away junior level web developer/software engineering jobs, its pretty obvious that, People should start looking for problems, brainstorm harder, and find innovative solution, instead of grinding leetcode mindlessly just for a software engineer job, build with a purpose, it's always good to think like a start-up founder and approach the problem with that mindset. While you have AI at your disposal, we must learn to use it efficiently, adapt to skills of the future, its high time we must learn prompt engineering and context engineering ,it's the ultimate key to unlock the true potential of AI.
